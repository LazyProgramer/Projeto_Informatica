Secured Roads

What is Secured Roads


Secured Roads is a project that seeks to improve the safety of the city of Aveiro by providing the community with information about the city roads in real-time. It alerts when there is traffic detected by ATCLL Sensors or something has been reported via twitter by the community. This is possible thanks to the support of Aveiro Open Lab and machine learning technology.

How it works


To provide authorities with the road's state in real-time, Secured Roads utilises the Smart Lamp Post (SLP) created and built by Aveiro Open Lab to check the state of the roads. To do this, it uses the information provided by the radars and cameras of the SLP to conclude if there is a traffic jam.
It can also notify them if someone has broken the law or if there has been an accident. It does this by utilising the radars and cameras provided by SLP as well as Twitter posts.
Thanks to all this, Secured Roads can make the roads of Aveiro just a bit safer.

How to use


Our alerts are published on the Aveiro Open Lab website Secured Roads Page and in the bot twitter account @securedroads.
If you wish to report some incident in Aveiro you could just tweet it or follow the #SecuredRoads twitter report format. If you don't follow the format make sure to include "Aveiro" in your tweet so that our bot can be sure that your report is from Aveiro.
